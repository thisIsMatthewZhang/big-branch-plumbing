# Professional Leak Detection & Repair

Water leaks can cause thousands in damage and waste precious resources. Big Branch Plumbing provides expert leak detection and repair services throughout the Northshore area, protecting your home and your wallet.

## What's Included

**Our leak repair services include:**

- **Leak Detection** - Advanced technology to locate hidden leaks
- **Pipe Repair** - Fix leaks in water supply lines
- **Fixture Leak Repair** - Repair leaking faucets, toilets, and connections
- **Slab Leak Repair** - Detect and repair leaks under concrete foundations
- **Wall & Ceiling Leak Repair** - Locate and fix leaks behind walls
- **Water Damage Prevention** - Solutions to prevent future leaks

## Common Leak Problems

- Hidden water leaks
- Dripping faucets
- Running toilets
- Water stains on walls or ceilings
- Mold growth
- High water bills
- Low water pressure
- Foundation cracks
